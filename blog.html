Impact of technology in Daily Life.

In every second of our life,technology plays a vital role.Without it,ofcourse we can live but life becomes so difficult. Now,if we see a 3 years child then, he can watch videos,open images and do a lot of stuffs with a mobile phone. He cries,if we try to keep him away.Obviously it has been playing an outstanding role to develop him personally as well because we can learn anything we want with the help of the internet.For me, it has been making more positive impacts in my life.

I want to be updated very fast about the world's latest news,current politics,sports, and everything.And it is just a matter of minutes I get updated with whatever I wanted to be. With the development of technology,quality of life has been increased immensely. I am hungry,I want something to eat then,I don't have to go anywhere it comes at my door in some minutes. Besides which,much more daily activities are possible without any physical efforts.This ultimately brings the enough satisfaction in my life. With the help of technology,learning ways ,skills development ,creativity everthing has been rapidly changed.Now, I can become professional, with enough knowledge sitting in my home by just watching tutorials from several sites.The Khanacademy,unacademy,Udemy these has made a great change in the life of any students from the average ones to the IITANS of India and MITANS of UnitedStates.It has been around 1 years,I have the Internet in my home,but I think my level would have been different if I had this facility since my +2 level.I would have made mine strong concepts on physics and chemistry seeing the videos from the many IIItans graduate professors from youtube. Besides, Better quality of life, longer lives due to modern medicine and technology, etc… Increased safety. Better police response times, GPS, surveillance/cameras, etc… Easier communication. Phones, internet, IM, email.Infact,it has increased overall efficiency in my life.

On the other hand,this technology has made me stuck into my comfort zone.A great dream suddenly comes in the mind,work for 2, 3 hours and then youtube comes,scrolling the news feed of facebook,instagram and everything gets vanished.Now, I am more engaged with my laptop than outside. Playing outdoor games has been almost finished.Probably making me and my body more lazy and unhealthy.The more technology becomes advanced,the more it seems to have control over our lives. The constant stream of the scenes on the video games, TV, movies and YouTube sometimes makes me causes an extra layer of stress, blurred vision and migraines. Technology is leading to social isolation which is characterized by a lack of contact with other people in normal daily living such as the workplace, with friends and even sometimes with my family.

Conclusion

Technology is like a coin which has both positive and negative sides. We are the deciders and we have to choose how to use it. The usage of technology for over exploitation of resources should be always avoided. If we use it for positive things, it will have positive effect of our lives and vice versa. Nobody would oppose the development of technologies in any sector but the developments should be in a positive way and they should not have any negative impact on present or future generations.
